PLA Rocket v1

In 2022 the rocket project started. The first rocket prototype was fabricated using a PVC pipe as casing and a 3D printed PLA nozzle. In this first model a convergent nozzle was utilized for its simplicity. The objetive was to make an initial working version with simple materials as the budget was very limited.

Aluminum Rocket v2

As the PLA nozzle was not able to withstand the heat from the combustion an upgraded metallic version was studied. The selected material was aluminium as it presents a significantly lower density while presenting great heat dissipation capabilities. Manufacturing a rocket model from Aluminium requires of more complex machinery. So, the parts were 3D printed by a third party manufacturer and then assembled together.
